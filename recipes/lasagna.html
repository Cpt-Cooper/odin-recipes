Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a italian dish that's made by lasagna noodles filling by: cheese, minced meat, ham and tomato sauce

Lasagna can be time-consuming, but the result are well worth the wait

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake for about 50 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
